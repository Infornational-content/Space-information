Fun Facts About Mercury

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • It has the shortest orbital period of all the planets in the Solar System.
  • Mercury has a very thin atmosphere, which cannot hold heat, leading to extreme temperature variations.
  • It is the smallest planet in the Solar System.
  • Mercury is named after the Roman messenger god.
  • The planet has a heavily cratered surface, similar to the Moon.
  • Mercury has a crust that is primarily composed of silicate rock.
  • It is classified as an inner planet due to its proximity to the Sun.
  • Mercury has an average core temperature of about 4,000 degrees Celsius.
  • The core of Mercury makes up about 75% of its radius, making it one of the largest cores relative to the size of the planet.
